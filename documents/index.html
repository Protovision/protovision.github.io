Welcome. I am Mark Swoope, a practitioner of computer programming since 2008.

My preferred programming languages are C and JavaScript because they are optimized, portable, and critical. Other programming languages I know are C++, Lua, and x86 Assembly.

I have chosen not to embrace any of the newer industry languages because I do not find them computationally or even syntactically superior to the existing languages they are attempting to replace.

I am currently working on my own, as yet to be released, programming language which I hope will make all other languages irrelevant for me. The primary principle for this language will be unconcealment of potentiality: applied as the transparency of the mutability within a program's state of both information and execution. From this single principle, many of today's and yesterday's language features are destroyed from inclusion in this newer, revaluated language.
